BUSINESS & TOKEN

In the integrated mobility ecosystem that ACARZ wants to create, a number of people involved in car-related businesses such as new car purchases, transportation, used, rental, insurance, credit card, maintenance, parts companies, etc. ACARZ induces their participation in ACARZ ecosystem, provides incentives to solve the problems of each service, and provides an incentive model that is applied differently for each vehicle service area.

01. User

Users register their car number and share photos and test drive information about the car. ACARZ Token will be paid as a reward for these actions.

Data such as user reviews act as information that helps other users use the service, and then spreads through information-sharing apps, etc., and the commerce cycle that leads back to purchase is voluntarily performed.

02. Demand for ACARZ Token

The platform's quote comparison and section partners such as new & used car purchase, scrap car, maintenance, supplies, rental, etc. will be the main consumers of the token.

Partners compete for promotion in order to receive more choices from users, and at this time, they purchase or consume ACARZ Tokens for prior exposure and promotion within the platform.

03. Incentive Pool

All participants contributing to the platform will receive ACARZ Token as an incentive through activities on the platform. These incentives are paid from the ACARZ incentive pool, and the incentive pool is provided through part of the platform fee and the reserve of the first issued token.

In order to stably pay incentives to platform participants, part of the fees generated by the platform are accumulated in the incentive pool, and part of the profits are distributed to the participants.

Q What is ACARZ ultimately trying to solve?

ACARZ aims to provide a new incentive structure for the mobility industry based on a long experience in the mobility market, and to provide all information related to the mobility life cycle quickly and accurately.

Q What is an ACARZ token?

ACARZ applies token economy to the vehicle life cycle to solve the problem of not being connected between the current centralized automobile industry and each service model. ACARZ tokens will be given as an incentive for participation in the ACARZ ecosystem, encouraging participation in the ACARZ ecosystem.

Q What is the ACARZ ecosystem?

In the integrated mobility ecosystem that ACARZ wants to create, a number of people who operate or use vehicle-related businesses such as new car purchases, transportation, used car companies, rental car companies, insurance companies, credit card companies, vehicle consumers, and parts companies are the owners. The ACARZ ecosystem introduces an incentive called ACARZ token to induce their participation in the ecosystem and to solve the problems of each service. We provide incentive models that are applied differently for each car service area.

Q What is the schedule of the future roadmap for ACARZ?

ACARZ aims to be a flexible mobility platform in which various participants collaborate. However, it is approaching in stages by a rapidly changing market. ACARZ is currently focusing on partnerships with various organizations in the mobility industry, and is developing with the aim of launching the ACARZ car information sharing platform in the fourth quarter of 21.
